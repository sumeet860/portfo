001 : Barcode based Student Attendance System

The Barcode based Student Attendance System is my final year project. The application generates the barcode for each students to make attendance easy.

This project is made to make attendance system easy in schools and institutions. Our system will generate the barcode for each and every student and when the student scans his/her barcode, attendance for them will be automatically updated in the portal.
